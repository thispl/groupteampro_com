Execution Procedure

Manpower Demand

Receiving Manpower requirement demand / JD from client

Service Level Agreement

Confirming Feasibility and SLA's (Service Level Agreements) between Client & TeamPRO

Compliance

Clients Confirms Legal Compliance for respective countries - India / Nepal / Sri Lanka /Philippines (as per attachment)

Appoint SPOC

TeamPRO initiates Execution and Allocates Exclusive SPOC - Establishes Single Point of Contact (SPOC for each Client)

Client Confirmation

SPOC will finalize recruitment plan and JD with client

Sourcing

Source candidates from candidate database

Screening

Initial screening and shortlisting of sourced candidates

initial Interview

Three rounds of assessment interview to shortlist
-Technical Interview
-HR interview by domain specialist
-Commercial Interview

Shortlisting

Prepare the candidate screening sheet along with all documents and master tracker

Client Submission

Submit the quality check (QC) cleared CV's to client

Client Review

CV review and shortlisting by client

Client Interview

Final Interview by client (direct / wired)
-Technical / trade or practical test (if required)

Selection

Final Selection by
Client

Job Offer

Offer acceptance and acknowledgment by candidate

Background Check

Candidate background check / ref.check (if required)

Medical

Medical examination & Preliminary documentation

Visa

Receipt of Visa & Visa Processing

Emigration

Emigration clearance and Travel arrangements

Ticket

Intimation of flight information to client and candidate

Orientation & Onboarding

Orientation and deployment of candidates
